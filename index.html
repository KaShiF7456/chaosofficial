— INTRODUCTION —

We began our journey in 2019 in PX as a FiveM roleplay gang and have since grown into a gaming community where roleplay, fun, and friendship come together. Our goal is to provide a place where members can enjoy gaming, chat, hang out, and build real connections in a positive and organized environment.

— OVERVIEW —

We focus on FiveM roleplay as our roots, but we also play a variety of other games to keep the community active and engaging. Alongside gaming, we host different activities and events to bring members closer — whether it's roleplay sessions, casual gaming nights, or community discussions.

Our server includes all the essential features such as music, moderation, and gaming bots to enhance the overall experience. We also maintain a leveling system that rewards members with roles and perks as they become more active, as well as special recognition for boosters, sponsors, and dedicated members.

Chaos Gang is not just a group of players — it is a family built on teamwork, respect, and the spirit of chaos. Our friendly moderation team ensures the community stays safe, interactive, and welcoming for everyone. If you are looking for roleplay, gaming, or simply a place to belong, this is the right server for you.

GANG RULES
Code of the Streets — Follow or Fall
01

Loyalty Above All

Your brothers come first. Betrayal is the only unforgivable sin in CHAOS GANG. Once you're in, your loyalty is unconditional.

02

Respect the Hierarchy

Every rank exists for a reason. Respect the chain of command. Orders from leadership are final — no arguments in the field.

03

RP at All Times

Stay in character at all times. No OOC drama in-game. Keep all real-world issues out of FiveM.

04

No Snitching

What happens in the gang stays in the gang. Never share information with outsiders, rivals, or law enforcement — IC or OOC.

05

Represent with Pride

You wear the CHAOS tag — act like it. No random killing, no embarrassing the gang. Every action reflects on all of us.

06

Activity Required

Members must be active. Go missing 14+ days without notice and your spot gets reviewed. We ride together or not at all.

07

No Hacking or Cheating

Zero tolerance for exploits, mods, or cheating of any kind. We earn our power. Violation = immediate removal.
